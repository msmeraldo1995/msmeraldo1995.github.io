## Hi, I'm Mark

I'm a data and analytics engineer focused on **operational and quality data in
regulated (GxP) environments**. I like the unglamorous middle of the stack —
the part where data from three different systems has to become one coherent
picture an executive can act on without a data team in the room.

### What I do

- **End-to-end analytics platforms.** Source extraction → canonical semantic
  layer → deterministic KPIs → AI-assisted insight → published reporting.
- **Pragmatic AI enablement.** I treat prompts as code: versioned,
  schema-constrained, and evaluated. I keep LLMs out of anything that has to be
  reproducible and in anything that benefits from pattern recognition.
- **Regulated-context engineering.** Traceability, reproducibility, and
  "no silent data loss" as first-class design constraints — not afterthoughts.

### How I work

I default to simple, inspectable tools (Python, Polars, DuckDB, Pydantic),
declarative configuration over bespoke code, and documentation that a future
maintainer — or a hiring manager — can actually follow. The
[architecture deep-dives](/docs/) on this site are written in that spirit.

### Let's talk

I'm open to conversations about analytics-engineering and data-platform roles.
